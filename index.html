Contact the Author
Dan Bunker has been playing guitar for more than twenty-three years. He spent two years playing and studying jazz composition at the University of Utah. Classical study and performance have also been a large part of his repertoire for many years. He has spent more than fifteen years in private teaching and educating hundreds of guitar students in the Salt Lake City area.

How hard are these hymns to play?
The arrangements in this book vary from beginner to the intermediate classical guitar player ability. However, even advanced players can use these arrangements as part of their repertoire.
Are the arrangements in Tab?
Each arrangement contains standard staff notation, tablature, and chord diagrams. You will find the music notation of your preference in this book.
How many hymn arrangements are in these books?
Volume 1 contains 20 hymns taken from the standard LDS Hymn Book and Volume 2 contains 26 hymns. These hymns have been arranged specifically for guitar.
Are there recordings of these hymns that I can hear or practice along with?
You can find the recordings for each Volume on the publishers website (Royal Cirque Publishing). Each hymn is recorded and performed in a slow tempo and the standard tempo to aid in practicing.
Each arrangement contains tablature, staff, and chord diagrams. See sample below.
